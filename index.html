About Me

I am a Process Engineer specialised in WWTP/RWZI design, sludge-line optimisation, anaerobic digestion, and industrial wastewater treatment. I have hands-on experience across the Netherlands, Italy, Switzerland, and Canada — delivering measurable improvements in biogas production, energy efficiency, and overall plant performance.

My work focuses on process troubleshooting, data-driven optimisation, aeration and energy efficiency, N/P removal pathways, SCADA interpretation, and industrial pretreatment technologies (ED/BPMED). I am familiar with Dutch frameworks including NEN-EN design standards, STOWA guidelines, and permitting procedures.

Open to engineering roles within utilities, waterschappen, consulting firms, and industrial treatment facilities across the Netherlands and Europe.
