About Me
About Me

I am an Environmental & Water Engineer with 6+ years of combined academic and industrial experience across the Netherlands, Italy, Switzerland, and Canada. I hold an M.Sc. in Environmental Engineering (TU Delft · Politecnico di Milano · FHNW — Erasmus) and a B.Sc. in Civil Engineering, which gives me a rare dual foundation: I work equally comfortably in water/wastewater process engineering, civil infrastructure, environmental compliance, and HSE-adjacent roles.

Most recently, as an Environmental Process Engineer at AtkinsRéalis (Toronto), I delivered CAD 45,000/year in operational savings, 5% biogas yield improvement, and authored tender documents, environmental permit applications, and O&M guidance for municipal and industrial wastewater infrastructure. Prior to that, at TU Delft, I designed and commissioned a pilot-scale BPMED electrodialysis system, achieving 12% recovery efficiency improvement within an EU Horizon 2020 research project.

I am comfortable across the full engineering lifecycle: concept design, feasibility studies, hydraulic & process modelling, permitting, tendering, field supervision, commissioning, O&M, performance optimisation, and data analysis. I communicate fluently with clients, regulators, contractors, and research collaborators, and I work well in multidisciplinary teams.

I am EU Permanent Resident (no visa required), based in Amsterdam, and open to roles across Europe, the Netherlands & Canada in water utilities & waterschappen, engineering consultancy, industrial facilities, HSE & compliance, environmental services, construction engineering, and municipal infrastructure.
